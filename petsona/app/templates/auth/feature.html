PetSona Features

Designed to promote responsible pet ownership through smart matching, education, and accessible pet services.

Pet to Owner Matching

PetSona analyzes pet characteristics such as behavior, energy level, and care requirements, then matches them with owners whose lifestyle and environment are suitable.

Owner to Pet Matching

Owners can also find pets based on their preferences, experience, living space, and availability. This ensures a long-term and healthy relationship between pets and owners.

Pet Knowledge & Education

PetSona provides reliable information about pet care, nutrition, health, and behavior to help owners make informed decisions and practice responsible pet ownership.

Nearby Pet Hotels & Services

Discover nearby pet hotels, clinics, grooming services, and other pet-related establishments based on location for convenience and peace of mind.
